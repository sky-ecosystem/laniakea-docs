# Hearth Commitments

The system's [telos point](../core-concepts/telos-point.md) — the irreducible commitment that all coordination orbits — is the protection of human life, of nature, and of the Earth. The specific details of the Telos Point must be developed alongside the Synome and fulfill the requirements of being widely held, genuinely good, and chosen for realistic, timely convergence rather than completeness.
