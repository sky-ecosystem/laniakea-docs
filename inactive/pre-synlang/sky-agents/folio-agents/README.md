# Folio Agents

Standardized supply-side holding structures in the Sky ecosystem. Folio Agents are rank 3 agents administered by a Prime — they are **not** a type of Halo. The key structural difference: a Halo wraps around a legal entity (Halo is the outer shell), while a folio is controlled BY the principal through legal entities (the principal's legal structure is the governance surface).

Each folio has a **principal** (the end user) who controls the folio through a **directive** (human language instructions). Two operating modes: **automated** (sentinel formation via guardian accord) or **principal control** (principal sentinel, direct operation).

Folio Agents are tokenless, single-owner, and instantly created. They serve as the entry point to the Sky Agent ecosystem, the required vehicle for Growth Staking participation, and the standardized way for professionals to access the supply side of the Sky ecosystem.
